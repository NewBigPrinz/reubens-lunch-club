About Us

Reuben's Lunch Club opened in 2021. Set in the heart of Sunny Rhyl, we aim to serve high quality lunches to our residents and visitors throughout the year. By adopting a canteen-style model, we've created a rotating menu that has something for everybody throughout the week.

Along with our daily hot specials, we also have a full range of cold sandwiches, baguettes and wraps available. We are also able to provide catering services. If you have an event you would like us to cater for, please contact us.

Fast

We're up with the gulls preparing our food ready for lunch time. While we can't guarantee a time, we do aim to serve you within 5 minutes of entering our canteen which is hopefully plenty of time to take a break and eat in.

Ecologically Sound

Where possible, we slow-cook our food, reducing our energy usage by approximately 25%. As we know exactly what we are cooking in advance, our food wastage is minimal. All of our containers for takeaway are fully recylable.

Delicious

Unless there is strong reason to do so (passata, frozen peas etc.), all of our ingredients are sourced fresh. We adapt any recipes as we see fit to local tastes, hence 'authenically inauthentic'.

Cost Effective

All of the previous statements made contribute to another aim of ours, providing high quality food at minimum prices. We ensure you can get a full, balanced meal with us for less than a large meal from a fast food restaurant.
